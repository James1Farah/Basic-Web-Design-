Star Wars Movies Original Trilogy

  • A New Hope (1977): The story begins as Luke Skywalker joins forces with the Rebel Alliance to destroy the Death Star, a powerful weapon of the Empire.
  • The Empire Strikes Back (1980): The Rebels face crushing defeat, and Luke learns a shocking truth about his father.
  • Return of the Jedi (1983): The final battle against the Empire sees the redemption of Darth Vader and the fall of the Emperor.

Prequel Trilogy

  • The Phantom Menace (1999): The origins of Anakin Skywalker, a young slave discovered by the Jedi, who shows great potential with the Force.
  • Attack of the Clones (2002): As the galaxy edges toward war, Anakin falls in love with Padmé Amidala and begins his dark descent.
  • Revenge of the Sith (2005): Anakin fully succumbs to the dark side, becoming Darth Vader and helping to establish the Empire.

Sequel Trilogy

  • The Force Awakens (2015): A new generation of heroes rises to face a resurgent dark side, now led by Kylo Ren.
  • The Last Jedi (2017): The Resistance fights for survival as Rey seeks to understand her connection to the Force.
  • The Rise of Skywalker (2019): The epic saga concludes with the final battle between the Resistance and the Sith.

Spin-offs and Expanded Universe

The Mandalorian: This hit Disney+ series follows a lone bounty hunter in the outer reaches of the galaxy, set after the fall of the Empire and before the rise of the First Order.

The Clone Wars: An animated series that delves into the events between Attack of the Clones and Revenge of the Sith, exploring the galaxy-wide conflict and the development of key characters like Anakin Skywalker and Ahsoka Tano.

Rogue One: A standalone film that tells the story of the Rebel spies who stole the plans for the Death Star, leading directly into the events of A New Hope.

Expanded Universe (Legends): Before Disney’s acquisition of Lucasfilm, many books, comics, and games expanded on the Star Wars story. These are now considered non-canon “Legends,” but still hold a special place in fans’ hearts.
