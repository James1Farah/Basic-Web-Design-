StarWars Movie Plot

This page provides a complete overview of the chronological story of the Star Wars saga, from the fall of the Republic to the final defeat of the Sith, creating a cohesive narrative for visitors who want to follow the epic journey from start to finish.

Episode I: The Phantom Menace (1999)

Plot: The story begins with Jedi Master Qui-Gon Jinn and his apprentice, Obi-Wan Kenobi, dispatched to resolve a trade dispute on the planet Naboo. They discover the sinister Sith are involved, and the situation escalates to an invasion. During their escape, they meet a young boy named Anakin Skywalker on the desert planet of Tatooine. Anakin shows great potential in the Force, and Qui-Gon believes he may be the Chosen One prophesied to bring balance to the Force. Meanwhile, Senator Palpatine begins his rise to power in the Galactic Senate. The film climaxes in a battle on Naboo, where the Sith apprentice, Darth Maul, is defeated, but Qui-Gon is killed. Anakin is taken under Obi-Wan’s tutelage.

Episode II: Attack of the Clones (2002)

Plot: Ten years later, the galaxy teeters on the edge of war as thousands of star systems threaten to secede from the Republic under Count Dooku's leadership. Senator Padmé Amidala narrowly escapes an assassination attempt and is assigned protection from Anakin, now a Jedi Knight. As they hide on Naboo, Anakin and Padmé fall in love. Meanwhile, Obi-Wan uncovers a secret clone army being created for the Republic and a mysterious plot to destroy the Jedi. The Clone Wars erupt when the Separatists attack and Jedi, alongside the clone army, battle the droid forces of Count Dooku. Anakin is haunted by visions of his mother's death and begins to struggle with his inner darkness.

Episode III: Revenge of the Sith (2005)

Plot: The Clone Wars rage across the galaxy, and Anakin Skywalker’s descent into darkness accelerates. Chancellor Palpatine, secretly the Sith Lord Darth Sidious, manipulates Anakin, convincing him that only the dark side of the Force can save Padmé, who he fears will die in childbirth. Anakin turns to the dark side, becoming Darth Vader, and helps Palpatine execute Order 66, which exterminates most of the Jedi. Obi-Wan confronts and defeats Anakin in a duel on Mustafar, leaving him gravely injured. Padmé dies after giving birth to twins, Luke and Leia. The Republic is transformed into the Galactic Empire, with Palpatine as Emperor, and Anakin, now encased in black armor, fully embraces his new identity as Darth Vader.

Solo: A Star Wars Story (2018)

Plot: Set before the events of A New Hope, this standalone film explores the origins of Han Solo, the iconic smuggler. Han escapes from a grim life on the planet Corellia and joins a gang of galactic criminals, meeting Chewbacca and Lando Calrissian along the way. Together, they undertake a dangerous mission to steal hyperfuel for the crime syndicate Crimson Dawn. The film highlights Han's development into the scoundrel we come to know, including his acquisition of the Millennium Falcon.

Rogue One: A Star Wars Story (2016)

Plot: Set directly before A New Hope, Rogue One tells the story of how the Rebel Alliance acquired the plans for the Death Star, the Empire's planet-destroying weapon. Jyn Erso, the daughter of Death Star scientist Galen Erso, leads a group of rebels on a daring mission to steal the plans from an Imperial base. The rebels succeed at the cost of their lives, but their sacrifice leads to the destruction of the Death Star in A New Hope. The final moments of the film directly connect to the opening of A New Hope, with the plans being handed to Princess Leia.

Episode IV: A New Hope (1977)

Plot: The story begins as Princess Leia, a leader in the Rebel Alliance, is captured by Darth Vader while attempting to smuggle the Death Star plans to the rebels. Farm boy Luke Skywalker, guided by Jedi Master Obi-Wan Kenobi, joins forces with smuggler Han Solo and his co-pilot Chewbacca to rescue Leia and deliver the plans to the Rebel Alliance. The film climaxes with a daring attack on the Death Star, in which Luke, using the Force, destroys the station, scoring a major victory for the rebels.

Episode V: The Empire Strikes Back (1980)

Plot: After the destruction of the Death Star, the Empire retaliates by driving the Rebel Alliance into hiding. Luke travels to the remote planet of Dagobah to train with Jedi Master Yoda, while Han Solo, Leia, and the others evade capture. Darth Vader is relentless in his pursuit of Luke, eventually trapping Han and Leia on Cloud City. In a shocking confrontation, Vader reveals that he is Luke's father. The film ends on a cliffhanger, with Han frozen in carbonite and Luke coming to terms with his lineage.

class="heading_style">Episode VI: Return of the Jedi (1983)

Plot: The Rebel Alliance plans a final assault on the Empire as the construction of a second Death Star nears completion. Luke, now a fully trained Jedi, attempts to redeem his father, Darth Vader, from the dark side. The rebels succeed in destroying the second Death Star, and Luke confronts the Emperor. In a final act of redemption, Vader turns on the Emperor to save Luke, but is mortally wounded in the process. The film concludes with the fall of the Galactic Empire and a celebration of peace across the galaxy.

Episode VII: The Force Awakens (2015)

Plot: Set 30 years after the fall of the Empire, the galaxy is now threatened by the First Order, a militant faction led by Supreme Leader Snoke and Kylo Ren, the son of Han Solo and Leia Organa. The Resistance, led by Leia, seeks Luke Skywalker, who has gone into exile. Rey, a scavenger on the desert planet Jakku, discovers her connection to the Force and helps the Resistance. With the help of Han Solo and others, she confronts Kylo Ren, and the Resistance destroys the First Order’s Starkiller Base, but Han is killed by his son.

Episode VIII: The Last Jedi (2017)

Plot: The Resistance is on the run from the First Order, which has all but destroyed their fleet. Rey finds Luke Skywalker and convinces him to train her in the ways of the Force. Meanwhile, Kylo Ren struggles with his pull between the dark side and the light. The film culminates in a desperate battle between the remnants of the Resistance and the First Order, with Luke projecting himself across the galaxy to face Kylo Ren in a final confrontation, allowing the Resistance to escape.

Episode IX: The Rise of Skywalker (2019)

Plot: The saga concludes with the return of Emperor Palpatine, who has been secretly orchestrating events from the shadows. Rey, revealed to be Palpatine’s granddaughter, grapples with her identity and her connection to the Force. The Resistance mounts a final battle against the Emperor’s forces. In the climactic showdown, Rey confronts Palpatine with the support of the spirits of past Jedi. With Kylo Ren’s redemption and sacrifice, the Sith are finally defeated. The Skywalker legacy ends with Rey adopting the Skywalker name and bringing hope to the galaxy.
